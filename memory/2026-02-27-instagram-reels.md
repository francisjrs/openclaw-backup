## 1. 🔵 Native Instagram Methods

### A. Professional Dashboard → Inspiration Tab
For **Professional/Creator accounts only**. The most niche-specific method available.

**Steps:**
1. Tap your profile picture → Profile
2. Tap **"Professional Dashboard"** → scroll to **"Your Tools"**
3. Tap **"Inspiration"** — shows Reels similar to your content, popular with your followers, and algorithmically suggested for you

> *"This method is my favorite because you can get very specific inspiration that matches your niche. You can also find trending audio using this method."*

Separate from the Inspiration tab, this gives a dedicated list of trending sounds.

**Steps:**

### C. Reels Trends Section (U.S. Only, Creator Accounts)
A dedicated hub showing currently trending audio clips *and* hashtags.

**Steps:**
1. Profile → Tap **three horizontal lines** (top right) → Settings and Activity
2. Tap **"Reels Trends"**
3. Browse trending sounds and hashtags; tap into each to see how creators are using them

> *"Look for reels templates from other creators — you'll see a 'Template by…' button on the bottom left if one exists."*

⚠️ **Note:** U.S. only. International creators should use the other methods.

### D. The Upward Arrow Icon (Most Universal Method)
Works on **all account types**, worldwide. The most commonly cited signal.

- While scrolling Reels, look for the **⬆️ upward-pointing arrow** next to the audio name at the bottom of a Reel
- This arrow = the audio is currently gaining traction / trending
- Tap the audio name → see total Reels count, preview how others used it, and hit **"Use Audio"** to start your own

> *"Look for the upward-pointing arrow next to audio names while scrolling and target tracks with under 5,000–10,000 uses to catch a trend before it reaches saturation."*
> — SocialPilot, https://www.socialpilot.co/blog/instagram-reels-trends

---

### E. Reel Creation → Templates → Trending
Available globally on all account types.

**Steps:**
1. Tap the **+** button → Choose **Reel**
2. Tap **"Templates"** at the top
3. Browse **"Recommended for You"** and **"Trending"** categories
4. Select a template, drop in your own clips, publish — done in minutes

Source: Buffer, https://buffer.com/resources/instagram-trending-reels/

### F. Explore Page (Manual Pattern Recognition)

## 3. 🎵 Trending Audio: Deep Dive

Audio is the #1 algorithmic signal on Reels. Instagram boosts videos using trending/rising audio.

### Method 1: The Arrow Icon (In-App, Universal)
- The **⬆️ arrow** next to an audio name = actively trending
- Best window: tracks with **under 5,000–10,000 uses** — early enough to ride the wave, not too early
- Once a track hits 500K+ uses, it's typically saturated
- Source: SocialPilot, https://www.socialpilot.co/blog/instagram-reels-trends
